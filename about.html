Typo Terminator

Made By: Luis Logan and Tyrone

This is a game in progress

Created for Ai Programming Class, we are making a typing speed excersise where you will get a series of ai generated words with typos from a personal dataset with an ai that we have created ourselves in order for the player to have to quickly type the correct spelling of that word.

Over time, the timer will get fater and faster and the words will get harder to read. Try to keep up as best you can and in the end you will get a score!
