Typo Terminator

A Project By: Luis, Logan, and Tyrone

Status: Development in Progress

Created for our AI Programming Class, Typo Terminator is a high-stakes typing challenge. The game utilizes a custom AI and personal dataset to generate a series of complex words—each intentionally scrambled with realistic typos.

Your mission is simple but difficult: Identify the original word and type the correct spelling before time runs out.

As you progress, the timer accelerates and the AI-generated distortions become increasingly harder to decipher. Keep your focus, maintain your speed, and see how high you can score before the typos take over!
